## Copyright infringement policy

`<BRAND_NAME>` takes intellectual property rights seriously. We respond to clear notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with applicable law.

## Reporting copyright infringement

If you believe that content on `<BRAND_NAME>` infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice. Your notice must include:

1. **Identification of the copyrighted work** you claim has been infringed.
2. **Identification of the infringing content** — including the URL or sufficient description to locate it on the platform.
3. **Your contact information** — name, address, telephone number, and email address.
4. **A statement** that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
5. **A statement** made under penalty of perjury that the information in your notice is accurate, and that you are the copyright owner or are authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
6. **Your physical or electronic signature**.

Send notices to: **dmca@`<DOMAIN>`**

## Our process

Upon receipt of a valid notice:

1. We will acknowledge receipt within **24 hours**.
2. We will evaluate the notice and, if valid, hide or remove the infringing content within **24 hours** of validation.
3. We will notify the user who posted the content.
4. The user may submit a counter-notice (see below).

## Counter-notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice including:

1. Identification of the removed content and its location prior to removal.
2. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the content was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.
3. Your name, address, telephone number, and a statement consenting to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for your district.
4. Your physical or electronic signature.

Upon receipt of a valid counter-notice, we will notify the original complainant. If the complainant does not file a lawsuit within **10–14 business days**, we may restore the content.

## Repeat infringers

`<BRAND_NAME>` will terminate accounts of users who are determined to be repeat infringers.
