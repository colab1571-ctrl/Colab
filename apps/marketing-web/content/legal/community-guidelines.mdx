## Our community

`<BRAND_NAME>` is a professional creative collaboration platform. Every person on this platform is here to create — not to scroll, not to perform, not to farm engagement. We expect every member to treat each other with the respect that creative professionals deserve.

These guidelines apply to all content and behaviour on the platform, including profiles, portfolio items, Vibe Check messages, workspace content, and direct communications.

## What we expect

### Be authentic
- Use a real identity. No impersonation of other artists, brands, or public figures.
- Represent your creative work honestly. Don't pass off others' work as your own.

### Be professional
- Treat every creator on the platform as a potential collaborator, not a transaction.
- Respond to Vibe Checks with respect, even when declining.
- Give constructive feedback — not cruelty disguised as honesty.

### Respect creative IP
- Do not share another creator's work without permission.
- Do not use the platform to steal, copy, or appropriate others' creative work.
- IP-safe collaboration means both parties retain rights to their own contributions.

### Keep it safe
- No harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- No content that sexualises, degrades, or dehumanises people.
- No discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, or national origin.

## Prohibited content

The following are not permitted on `<BRAND_NAME>`:

- **Hate speech**: Content that dehumanises people based on protected characteristics.
- **Harassment**: Repeated unwanted contact, threats, or intimidation of any user.
- **Sexual content**: Explicit sexual content is not permitted in profiles or workspaces.
- **Violent content**: Content that depicts or encourages real-world violence.
- **Spam**: Unsolicited commercial messages, repeated identical Vibe Checks, or other spam behaviour.
- **Misinformation**: False claims about your identity, credentials, or creative work.
- **Illegal content**: Any content that violates applicable law, including copyright infringement, CSAM, or content that promotes illegal activity.

## IP and copyright

If you believe your creative work has been used without permission, you can:
1. Use the Report button on any content to alert our moderation team.
2. Submit a formal DMCA takedown notice at [/legal/dmca](/legal/dmca).

## Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:
- A warning
- Content removal
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban and account deletion

Enforcement decisions are made by our moderation team according to our risk-tiered moderation process. You may appeal enforcement actions by contacting **support@`<DOMAIN>`**.

## Reporting

If you encounter content or behaviour that violates these guidelines:
1. Use the **Report** button on the content or profile.
2. Select the appropriate reason.
3. Add optional context.

Our moderation team reviews reports according to the following SLAs:
- Harassment / threats: 4h acknowledgement, 24h resolution
- IP / DMCA: 24h acknowledgement, 7 days resolution
- General reports: 48h acknowledgement, 7 days resolution
