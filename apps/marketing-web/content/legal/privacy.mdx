This Privacy Policy explains how `<BRAND_NAME>` ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our Service. ## 2. Data We Collect

**Account data**: Email address, password (hashed), display name, date of birth (for age verification), location (city-level).

**Profile data**: Portfolio items (images, audio, video), bio, vocation categories, professional experience, social media connections (Instagram, YouTube, Spotify — only if you connect them).

**Usage data**: Features used, pages visited, collaboration activity, search queries. Collected via PostHog analytics (product analytics, session replay, feature flags).

**Device data**: Device type, operating system, browser, IP address (hashed where stored).

**Payment data**: Processed by Stripe (web) or Apple/Google (mobile). We do not store full payment card details.

**Collaboration data**: Chat messages, files shared, workspace activity. Stored with immutable audit logs for IP-safety.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- To provide and improve the Service

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with:
