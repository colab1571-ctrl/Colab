## Key Terms

### 1. Parties

- **Disclosing Party**: Colab Inc. (or legal entity name, TBD)
- **Receiving Party**: Beta participant (individual creator)

### 2. Confidential Information

Confidential information includes all information disclosed to the Receiving Party in connection with the beta program, including but not limited to:

- Unreleased software features, UI/UX designs, product roadmap
- Pricing structures, subscription tiers, and monetization strategy
- Platform name and brand identity before public announcement
- Technical architecture, algorithms, and implementation details
- User data and analytics from the beta period
- Marketing materials not yet publicly released
- Business strategy, partner relationships, and financial information

### 3. Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

- Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
- Not publish screenshots, recordings, or descriptions of the platform on social media, blogs, or any public forum
- Use Confidential Information only for the purpose of participating in the beta and providing feedback
- Notify Colab Inc. immediately upon discovering any unauthorized disclosure

**Note on AI mockup screen**: The platform includes an AI image generation feature that displays a system-level overlay and blocks screenshots on Android (FLAG_SECURE). This technical measure supplements but does not replace the NDA obligation.

### 4. Exclusions from Confidentiality

The following are NOT considered Confidential Information:

- Information that is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party
- Information the Receiving Party possessed before the beta program began (documentable)
- Information independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to Confidential Information
- Information disclosed with Colab Inc.'s prior written consent

### 5. Intellectual Property

- Beta participants retain full ownership of any creative content they upload to the platform during the beta period, consistent with the platform's Terms of Service
- Feedback, suggestions, and bug reports submitted by beta participants become the property of Colab Inc. and may be incorporated into the product without compensation
- The platform, its code, design, and all proprietary technology remain the exclusive property of Colab Inc.

### 6. Term

- Agreement is effective from the date of signing
- Confidentiality obligations survive for **12 months** after the end of the beta program
- "End of beta program" = date Colab Inc. notifies participants that the beta has concluded

### 7. Beta Build Disclaimer

Beta participants acknowledge:

- The beta software may be unstable, contain bugs, and may change or be discontinued at any time
- Colab Inc. provides the beta software "as is" without warranty
- Beta participants use the software at their own risk
- Colab Inc. is not liable for any data loss, device issues, or other problems arising from beta participation

### 8. Governing Law

- Governing law: [State/Province TBD] — match legal entity jurisdiction
- Dispute resolution: Binding arbitration per AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules (or jurisdiction-appropriate equivalent)

## Signing Process

1. Legal counsel reviews and finalizes NDA text.
2. Company representative signs master NDA template (wet or electronic signature).
3. DocuSign envelope created with beta participant's name and email pre-filled.
4. Envelope sent to participant with 5-day signing deadline.
5. Signed NDA stored in DocuSign for 7 years.
6. Only participants with completed NDA receive TestFlight / Play Alpha invite links.
